Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 large bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 12 oz bag of milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Beat together sugar, butter and eggs
  3. In a large bowl, mix together bananas, vanilla, flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder
  4. Add dry ingredients into wet ingredients and beat altogether
  5. Mix in chocolate chips
  6. Scoop batter into muffin tins and bake for 25 minutes
